Strategic Playbook

Priority Action Matrix

Data-driven interventions ranked by impact and implementation urgency to recover the estimated $295K annual revenue loss.

🔴 Critical Priority
"Year 1" Onboarding Playbook
Automated email sequence: Day 7 welcome + tips, Day 30 usage check-in, Day 60 feedback + offer, Month 6 loyalty discount. Targets the 47.44% first-year churn window directly.
🎯 0–12 month customers
🔴 Critical Priority
Contract Migration Campaign
Offer "13th Month Free" or 15% annual discount. Surface the offer at Month 3 and Month 9 — the highest-conversion windows. A 15× churn reduction by moving to 2-year contracts.
🎯 Month-to-month subscribers
🟠 High Priority
Auto-Pay Migration Incentive
Offer $5/month permanent discount for switching from electronic check to bank transfer or credit card. Removes the #1 payment friction point at far lower cost than customer acquisition.
🎯 Electronic check users
🟠 High Priority
Tech Support Bundling
Include 3-month free Tech Support trial in all Fiber Optic plans. Convert at Month 4 at reduced rate. Directly targets the ~42% churn rate segment with a proven 26-point reduction mechanism.
🎯 Fiber Optic subscribers
🟡 Medium Priority
Senior Customer Tier
Simplified billing, dedicated support line, optional paper statements. Senior customers show ~42% churn rate and respond well to personalized service differentiation.
🎯 SeniorCitizen = 1 segment
🟡 Medium Priority
High-ARPU Retention Offer
Proactive price-match offer triggered at 45-day inactivity for high-value accounts. Prevents cancellation before it happens for customers paying $74+/month — the highest-revenue cohort.
🎯 Customers paying $74+/month
